We have games!

The Supply Chain Sandbox has developed board and video games to teach supply chain principles, and so have others.

  • Supply Chain Jenga - The tower game you love and fear, with new gameplay and rules!
  • Supply Chain Sprint - A classic 8-bit game, updated to reflect the relay race that is modern supply chains.
  • Beer Game App - There are several online variations on the classic MIT Beer Supply Chain Game. This is our favorite, others are linked in the Resources section below.
